To make an allo fry

Ingredients:

allo
onion
oil
masala powder
chilli powder

Procedure:

1. Peel the potatos
2. Cut the potatos into pieces (shape is as per user interest)
3. cut the onions
4. take a pan and pour oil into it and wait till it gets hot
5. place the pieces of onion and potato into the pan
6. deep fry it
7. remove the remaining oil 
8. add chilli and masala powder to the pieces

Done Your potato fry is complete.
